About Us

Ereto Namunyak is a local organization with a membership of 10 drawn from a wide range of professions. Ereto Namunyak was formed in 2024 and registered in 2025 as a community-based organization under the laws of Kenya. The vision of Ereto Namunyak is to have a rising, prosperous Maa community where everyone has a share and a role to play. The mission is to fight hunger, drought, illiteracy, and bad governance. The formation of the organization was precipitated by the realization among professionals that there were deficiencies regarding socio-economic development in the community, particularly in education, infrastructure, human resource development, and land/pastoral resource base protection. Consequently, the core problems that were the focus of the organization’s efforts from the onset were low education standards, spearheading the rights of persons with special needs, primary health care, and educating the society to acknowledge their rights and responsibilities. Ereto-Namunyak's operations cover the whole of Magadi, Shompole and Olkeri community land, Oldonyo Nyokie and Olkiramatian group ranches.

Key Programs

Our Mission

To bring change among the group and the community at large, specifically to:

  • Enlighten the community on matters pertaining to the society, making good use of readily available resources to create wealth in the community.
  • Fight hunger, drought, illiteracy, and bad governance.
  • Create awareness in this community on matters of education, church empowerment, FGM, and HIV/AIDS.
  • Educate society to acknowledge their rights and responsibilities.
  • Spearhead the rights of persons with special needs.
