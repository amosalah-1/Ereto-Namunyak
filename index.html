About Us

Ereto Namunyak is a local organization with a membership of 10 drawn from a wide range of professions. Ereto Namunyak was formed in 2024 and registered in 2025 as a community based Organization under the laws of Kenya. The vision of Ereto Namunyak then was to have a rising, prosperous Maa community where everyone has a share and a role to play. The mission then was to fighting hunger Drought illiteracy and bad governance etc. The formation of the organization was precipitated by the realization among professionals that there were deficiencies regarding socio-economic development in the community, particularly in education, infrastructure, human resource development, and land/Pastoral Resource base protection.
